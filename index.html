About Us


KNC Branding was founded in 2017. We are optimized as a small signage company yet we have the accumulated knowledge and experience +30 years of working in the industry. We manufacture various forms of signage ranging from banners and flags to large shopfront signboards and 3D fabrication. Through both our sign industry experience, the insight gained through understanding the needs and requirements of all of our customers , KNC will ensure that we continue to offer high levels of service and an overriding commitment to quality and innovation.
